Your Privacy Their Benefit

by Juan D Carballo Sanchez

Introduction

       With the rise of an interconnected system of electronics, technology has become a fundamental aspect of many individualistic lifestyles; with a constant surge focused on transforming, assimilating, and connecting everything to the web. This desire to advance has subsequently allowed any one individual to store and manage a majority of their personal information on a network, be it from a social platform, banking, or a health care system; making it easily accessible and transferable when needed. Technologly has surley provided us a safe and reliable way to trasnfer personal data, or has it?

       Well first lets define personal information, personal infromat regarded in this context is defined as any info needed to identify, contact, or locate a single person; this can range from simple items such as a full name to more complex ones such as genetic or biometric data. Why is this important? Well with the assembly of the internet, we have also seen the start up and advancement of various connection, and surveillance programs which have led many individuals to question just how much privacy is available in today's world. Furthermore, these programs have raised many ethical and legal issues amongst companies and consumers on the processing of private information and its relation to the distribution of such data to other sectors or governing bodies.

       Facebook for example saw tremendous market value loss in March of 2018, after revelations concerning the dissemination of information related to individuals not connected to a particular group or applet. This meant if you were friends with someone on Facebook and they used a specific application then not only did they willingly allow the access of their information by the organization in question but they also granted access to yours as well. Corporations and agencies should not have the right to use technology with this intent of distributing or monitoring personal information unless explicitly allowed by the individual themselves. Also, a safe infrastructure must be sustained to prevent such acts from occurring. It goes against ones own autonomy to be monitored or have their data spread around regardless of its anonymity to the receiving sector.
